## Featured Projects

### Clinical Outcome Predictor — 1,300 Patient Records
**Python | Pandas | Scikit-learn | Matplotlib | Seaborn**

End-to-end analysis of a multidimensional clinical dataset covering 
7 diagnoses to identify the key patient, laboratory, and vital sign 
factors that predict whether a patient recovers, remains under 
treatment, or dies. Includes data pipeline engineering, clinical 
risk threshold feature engineering, EDA across 6 analytical 
questions, and a Random Forest classifier with feature importance 
analysis interpreted from both a statistical and clinical perspective.

Creatinine emerged as the strongest predictor of mortality — 
consistent with its role as a systemic health indicator across 
all 7 diagnoses.
